**Learning objective:** By the end of this exercise, students will be able to compile research findings into a well-organized document that summarizes their insights and discoveries about cloud-native firewalls.

## Demonstrate your findings

Create a Google Doc to compile your findings, ensuring it is well-organized and easy to navigate. Ensure the document can be viewed publicly so your instructor can access it. Use a clear and professional format, with headings, subheadings, and bullet points, to make it easy to read.

You'll use this document to summarize your insights and discoveries from your research into a cohesive report on cloud-native firewalls.

Your report should include:

- An overview of the firewall offerings from the cloud providers you investigated.

- Your comparative analysis of cloud firewalls vs on-premise solutions.

- Your evaluation of specific use cases where cloud firewalls provide value.

- Your overall conclusions and recommendations around cloud firewall adoption.

As you compile your report, focus on creating a narrative that showcases your journey of discovery and the key lessons learned. Aim to produce a resource that demonstrates your expertise and could serve as a valuable reference for others exploring cloud firewall solutions.

While guidance has been provided for each exercise, feel free to adapt or extend your research based on your own interests and learning style. The most important outcome is that you come away with a deep, practical understanding of how firewalls function in the cloud and how they can be effectively leveraged in modern network architectures.
