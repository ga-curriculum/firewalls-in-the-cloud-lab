## Lab requirements

The following requirements describe the necessary deliverables for the Firewalls in the Cloud Lab.

| Criteria                                                                                                                                     | Points |
| -------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | ------ |
| The **cloud firewall research document** was shared publicly so that it can be viewed by the instructor on their first attempt when grading. | 10     |
| The **cloud firewall research document** includes an overview of the firewall offerings from the cloud providers you investigated.           | 10     |
| The **cloud firewall research document** includes an evaluation of specific use cases where cloud firewalls provide value.                   | 10     |
| The **cloud firewall research document** includes conclusions and recommendations around cloud firewall adoption.                            | 10     |
| The **cloud firewall research document** is formatted in a clear manner.                                                                     | 4      |
| The **cloud firewall research document** uses clear and professional language.                                                               | 5      |
