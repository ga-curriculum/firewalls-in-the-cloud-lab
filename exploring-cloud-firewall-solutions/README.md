## Research cloud providers' firewall solutions

In this exercise, your task is to investigate the native firewall solutions offered by at least two major cloud platforms of your choice, such as AWS, Azure, GCP, Oracle Cloud, or IBM Cloud.

Dive into each provider's official documentation and resources to identify their specific firewall services, key features, and potential use cases.

Organize your findings into a concise document summarizing each provider's firewall offerings, highlighting unique capabilities or architectures. Aim to create a resource that you can refer back to when making decisions about cloud firewall implementations.
